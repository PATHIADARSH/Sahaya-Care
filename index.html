What is Sahaya Care?

Sahaya Care is an early-stage digital layer that sits on top of your existing hospital processes to make patient journeys smoother and staff coordination easier – starting with simple, high-impact workflows.

🤝

Patient support hub

Central place for patients and attendants to raise non-clinical requests, track status, and get quick updates from hospital support teams.

📋

Task & coordination

Simple, role-based task views for billing, reception, nursing, and facility teams to ensure requests are not missed and are resolved on time.

📊

Operational visibility

Snapshot of volumes, turnaround time, and bottlenecks so administrators can see exactly where support processes need improvement.

Why hospitals are exploring Sahaya Care

Built in close conversation with hospital stakeholders, Sahaya Care focuses on small but meaningful improvements that add up to a better patient experience and more efficient teams.

Key value for your hospital:
  • ✔Improve patient and attendant satisfaction without large IT investments.
  • ✔Give staff a clearer view of requests and handovers across shifts.
  • ✔Start with a single department or floor and expand gradually.
  • ✔Flexible to adapt to your existing SOPs and internal processes.
